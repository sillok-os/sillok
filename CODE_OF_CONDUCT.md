# Code of Conduct

This project adopts **Contributor Covenant version 2.1** as its Code of Conduct.

The full text is maintained at the official source:

> <https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/>

By participating in this project (issues, discussions, pull requests, release events), you agree to abide by that document.

## Reporting

Report concerns privately to the project maintainer at **<tykim21@gmail.com>**. Reports are reviewed in confidence within 5 business days. The maintainer may delegate review to a future Steering Group once formed (see [`GOVERNANCE.md`](GOVERNANCE.md)).

## Enforcement

Enforcement guidelines follow the four-step ladder defined in Contributor Covenant 2.1 §Enforcement: (1) correction, (2) warning, (3) temporary ban, (4) permanent ban. The maintainer is the final authority during the pre-Steering-Group phase of the project.
